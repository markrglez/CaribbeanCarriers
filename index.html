Our Mission

Our mission at Caribbean Carriers is simple: to remove all the headaches and uncertainty from exporting goods to Cuba. Whether you're shipping electronics, auto parts, motorcycles, e-bikes, agricultural products or personal vehicles, we handle every step—from pickup and export licensing to ocean freight and final delivery at the Port of Mariel.

We believe in complete transparency and compliance. That means clear, upfront quotes, proactive communication throughout the journey, and strict adherence to U.S. export regulations and Cuban customs requirements. You never have to chase down paperwork or worry about hidden fees—our team coordinates directly with Customs, carriers, and local authorities so your cargo moves smoothly and arrives on time.

Above all, we're committed to your peace of mind. By bundling logistics, documentation, and shipping into a single turnkey solution, we let you focus on what matters most—growing your business—while we worry about the details. At Caribbean Carriers, exporting to Cuba has never been easier.

How It All Works

Step 1: Request a Quote

Begin by selecting the items you want to export from our product list and request a quote. If you have specific requirements, feel free to submit a detailed Request for Quotation form available on our site.

Step 2: Personalized Follow‑Up

Within 24 hours of receiving your quote request, we'll reach out to discuss the details and assist you with the next steps.

Step 3: Export Licensing

Rest assured, all exports to Cuba are conducted under U.S. Export Licensing regulations from the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS).

Step 4: Bulk Order and Payment

Purchases are processed as bulk orders and payment must be completed before shipment to streamline compliance protocols.

Step 5: Payment Options

Payments are accepted in USD via website checkout, cash, money orders, wire transfers, or BTC for your convenience.

Step 6: Shipping and Delivery

Orders ship from Miami, FL directly to the Port of Mariel, Cuba.
