How Vehicle Export Works

Step 1: Gather Documentation

Gather your vehicle title (or lien release) & bill of sale. These documents are essential for the export process.

Step 2: Complete Required Forms

Download and complete the Letter of Intent and Power of Attorney forms. These forms must be filled out and emailed to caribbeantransport001@gmail.com

Letter of Intent Power of Attorney

Step 3: Select Shipping Method

Choose your preferred shipping method:

  • RoRo (Roll-on/Roll-off) – Drive-on/drive-off service. Fast & economical.
  • Container (20' or 40') – Enclosed shipping, best for classic or high-value vehicles.

Step 4: Export Clearance

We handle all export clearance with U.S. Customs & Border Protection (CBP) and the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS).

Step 5: Shipping & Delivery

Your vehicle ships from Miami, FL to the Port of Mariel, Cuba. Average transit time is 3-6 weeks.
